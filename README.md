Portfolio Website Overview
Your portfolio website is designed to showcase your professional identity in a clean and user-friendly way. It consists of two main pages:


Home Page

Serves as the introduction to your portfolio.
Highlights your name, role, and a brief summary of your skills or expertise.
Can include a hero section with a tagline, a short bio, and links to your work or social profiles.
Purpose: Create a strong first impression and give visitors a quick overview of who you are.



Contact Page

Provides an easy way for visitors to reach you.
Includes a contact form (name, email, message) or direct links to your email and social media.
Purpose: Encourage networking and collaboration by making communication simple and accessible.
